Green Future Symposium: Shaping Sustainable Agriculture at CZU

Authour: Rawan Hababa

The Green Future Symposium at CZU recently brought together students, faculty, and industry experts to explore innovations in sustainable agriculture. Through keynote speeches, workshops, and hands-on demonstrations, attendees discussed ways to tackle pressing environmental challenges, from climate-resistant crop strategies to reducing agricultural waste. The event emphasized the need for collaboration in creating a more sustainable food system, inspiring students to engage in research and apply eco-friendly practices. The symposium highlighted CZU's commitment to leading the way in sustainable agriculture and encouraged participants to become active contributors to a greener future.
