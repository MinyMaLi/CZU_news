Greenhouse Innovation: A CZU Student Project Revolutionizing Urban Farming

Authour: Ahmed Fouda

A group of CZU students is working on an exciting project to design a new type of greenhouse for urban farming. Using solar panels and recycled water systems, their greenhouse model makes it easier to grow fresh food in city areas while using fewer resources. This project shows how urban spaces can be turned into green areas that produce food in an eco-friendly way. The students hope their work will inspire others to try sustainable farming in cities and help reduce the environmental impact of food production.
